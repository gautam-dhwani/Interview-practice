## Interview Tips Recap

### Technical Questions
1. **Clarify first** - "Let me make sure I understand..."
2. **Think out loud** - share your reasoning
3. **Start simple** - basic solution, then optimize
4. **Test your code** - walk through examples
5. **Discuss trade-offs** - time/space complexity, scalability

### System Design Questions
1. **Gather requirements** - users, scale, features
2. **Draw high-level** - start with boxes
3. **Deep dive** - explain components
4. **Discuss bottlenecks** - where might it fail?
5. **Scale it** - how to handle 10x traffic?

### Behavioral Balance
- **Be honest** - "I haven't worked with X, but I'd approach it like..."
- **Show learning** - "When I learned Y, I..."
- **Ask questions** - shows engagement and thinking
